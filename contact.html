Walking/Cycling

Exit Oslo Central Station and head west on Jernbanetorget toward Stortingsgata. Turn left onto Stortingsgata and continue walking. Follow Stortingsgata until you reach Karl Johans gate; turn right onto Karl Johans gate. Walk along Karl Johans gate, passing through the Royal Palace grounds. Continue on Karl Johans gate until you reach the intersection with Inkognitogata.Turn left onto Inkognitogata, and follow it as it becomes Bogstadveien.Keep walking along Bogstadveien until you reach Majorstuen.
